Flood Preparedness Guide

Plan ahead to protect your family, home, and valuables during floods.

Before a Flood

  • Know your area's flood risk and evacuation routes.
  • Move valuables and electrical equipment to higher floors.
  • Prepare sandbags if advised by authorities.

During a Flood

  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.
  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Follow official updates and evacuation orders.

After a Flood

  • Wait for authorities to declare it safe before returning home.
  • Clean and disinfect everything that got wet.
  • Watch out for snakes, insects, and contaminated water.
