Fire Safety Guide

Be prepared for fire emergencies and learn how to respond effectively.

Before a Fire

  • Install smoke alarms on every level of your home.
  • Test alarms monthly and change batteries yearly.
  • Plan two ways out of each room and practice fire drills.

During a Fire

  • Stay low to avoid smoke and crawl to the nearest exit.
  • Stop, Drop, and Roll if your clothes catch fire.
  • Call emergency services once you are safely outside.

After a Fire

  • Do not enter your home until authorities declare it safe.
  • Contact insurance and document damages.
  • Seek medical care for burns or smoke inhalation.
