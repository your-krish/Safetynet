Earthquake Drill

  1. Announce "DRILL START" to all participants.
  2. Everyone performs Drop, Cover, Hold On for 30 seconds.
  3. Evacuate calmly to your safe meeting point.
  4. Count everyone and ensure no one is missing.
  5. Debrief after drill: what went well, what to improve.
