Module 1: Disaster Preparedness Basics

Understand the fundamentals of disaster planning, risk assessment, and emergency actions.

Lesson 1: What is Disaster Preparedness?

Disaster preparedness means taking steps before an event occurs to reduce risks, save lives, and protect property. This includes creating emergency plans, preparing kits, and staying informed.

Lesson 2: Creating a Family Emergency Plan

  • Identify safe places in your home for each type of disaster.
  • Assign meeting points if you get separated.
  • List important contacts and share with family members.
  • Practice evacuation routes at least twice a year.

Lesson 3: Building a Basic Emergency Kit

Prepare a kit that includes:

  • Water (3 liters per person per day)
  • Non-perishable food
  • First-aid supplies
  • Flashlight, batteries, and portable charger
  • Copies of important documents
