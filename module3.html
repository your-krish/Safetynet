Module 3: Response & Recovery

Learn what to do during a disaster and how to recover quickly afterward.

Lesson 1: Immediate Response

During a disaster, focus on safety first:

  • Follow your emergency plan and evacuation routes.
  • Call emergency numbers (100, 101, 102, 108) as needed.
  • Provide first aid to injured persons if safe to do so.

Lesson 2: Post-Disaster Safety

  • Check for hazards like gas leaks, unstable structures, and live wires.
  • Listen to official updates on radio or verified social media channels.
  • Do not re-enter unsafe buildings until cleared by authorities.

Lesson 3: Recovery & Mental Health

Recovery can take time. Take care of mental well-being:

  • Reach out for psychological support if needed.
  • Document damage for insurance claims and relief applications.
  • Join community rebuilding efforts to restore normalcy.
