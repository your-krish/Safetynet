Module 2: Family & Community Planning

Learn how to create a household plan and work with your community to stay safe.

Lesson 1: Household Safety Plan

Develop a clear plan that includes evacuation routes, shelter locations, and communication methods.

  • Write down emergency contacts and keep copies in easy-to-find spots.
  • Designate a “safe room” for storms or earthquakes.
  • Assign responsibilities to each family member (turning off utilities, gathering kits, etc.).

Lesson 2: Neighborhood Coordination

Disasters affect entire neighborhoods, so it’s critical to work together.

  • Create a neighborhood WhatsApp/Telegram group for quick alerts.
  • Identify neighbors who may need special assistance (elderly, disabled, kids).
  • Share emergency contacts and resources with nearby households.

Lesson 3: Practice Drills

Practice makes perfect! Conduct family and neighborhood drills at least twice a year.

  • Run through fire evacuation and earthquake drop-cover-hold exercises.
  • Practice calling emergency numbers quickly.
  • Time your evacuation to improve response speed.
