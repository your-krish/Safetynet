Earthquake Safety Guide

Learn what to do before, during, and after an earthquake to keep you and your family safe.

Before an Earthquake

  • Secure heavy furniture and appliances to walls.
  • Identify safe spots under sturdy tables and away from windows.
  • Prepare an emergency kit and family contact plan.

During an Earthquake

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On until shaking stops.
  • Stay indoors if you are inside — avoid running outside.
  • If outdoors, move away from buildings, trees, and power lines.

After an Earthquake

  • Check yourself and others for injuries.
  • Be prepared for aftershocks.
  • Inspect your home for gas leaks or structural damage before re-entering.
