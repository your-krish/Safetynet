Emergency Contacts

Save these important contacts to get help quickly during a disaster.

Dial 100 for immediate police assistance.

Fire Department

Dial 101 for fire emergencies.

Ambulance

Dial 102 for medical emergencies.

Disaster Management Helpline

Dial 108 for disaster management support.

Women Helpline

Dial 1091 for women-related emergencies.
